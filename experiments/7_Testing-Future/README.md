# The Future of (AI?) Testing

> This section is covered in slides — no hands-on experiments.

## Topics Covered

- **Is AI a bubble?** — the dot-com parallel: losers didn't believe, didn't learn, didn't adapt fast enough
- **Gartner Hype Cycle** — AI-augmented testing on the "Slope of Enlightenment", plateau in 2-5 years (2025 update)
- **Autonomous testing levels** — from no automation (level 0) through tester assistance, partial automation, conditional automation, to full autonomy (level 5)
- **QA maturity layers** — manual & exploratory → scripted automation → orchestration & quality gates → AI-augmented → agentic/autonomous
- **The tester's evolving role** — which skills become critical as AI takes over more of the testing toil
